ABOUT

I am currently pursuing my Master's at Philip Merrill College of Journalism. I graduated from the University of Maryland in May 2018 with a B.A. in Government and Politics. I am an aspiring multimedia journalist and graphic designer. My diverse college and internship experiences, as well as my international background, helped to develop in me a fascination for approaching situations and problems from many points of view, and to foster a mindset for treating complex stories with the delicacy and fairness they demand.
